MIT GDC Team Members

Groups

The MIT GDC Team consists of MIT Faculty, Research Staff, Center Staff, Visiting Scholars and Scientists, Affiliated Researchers, Undergraduate Students, and Graduate Students. These Honored Team Members care deeply about properly architecting and engineering the next-generation geospatial platform for public participation and benefit. The Team is organized into twelve groups:

  • Software Architecture Group
  • Software Design Group
  • Software Development Group
  • Geonumerics Group
  • Internet of Things Group
  • Intelligent Engineering Group
  • High Performance Computing Geospatial Platform Group
  • Research Policy Group
  • Administration Group
  • Operations Group
  • Technical Writing Group
  • Scientific Writing and Communications Group
